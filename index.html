About

Farm to Fork

Our mission is simple:
Deliver the highest-quality farm-fresh produce, right to your doorstep.
We love delicious, ripe produce sourced from the finest, local farms. But, we discovered it’s not always easy or accessible to go to a farmers market or scout out a local farm stand. With all the uncertainty in the world right now, one thing is clear; having direct access to fresh, healthy produce is essential. We started Farm to Fork to help support our food chain suppliers sell their growing surplus and allow everyone to access delicious and local health foods.

When you buy healthy food from community farmers, everybody wins. That’s why all of our products are produced by local, certified organic farmers that use sustainable practices to help feed their communities. We gather high-quality meat and produce from farms in our network and make it available to you through our simple, online ordering system. Select what you want and how you want it delivered, and we’ll handle the rest!

Let us connect you to the best ingredients we can grow.
